# ClearingHouse ClearingHouse is a protocol that allows for asset issuance and p2p trading on the [Viacoin](http://viacoin.org) blockchain. Viacoin is a particularly fast digital currency which was designed to be the data-carrier for ClearingHouse, 25x faster than Bitcoin. It was released on 11th August 2014. You can read more about it on the Viacoin Blog: http://blog.viacoin.org/2014/08/11/1-clearinghouse.html ## XCH XCH is the native currency of ClearingHouse and is required to issue crypto assets and stocks. XCH cannot be mined. For a period of ~45 days starting 11th August 2014 Viacoins can be used to initialize XCH. The conversion period will end on approximately 28th September 2014 after block 251100 is mined. ## Clearwallet [Clearwallet.co](https://clearwallet.co) is a web implementation that takes advantage of the ClearingHouse protocol. <iframe width="560" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/uIOjWI3tNr0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe> ## Decentralized Exchange Trade your own assets, tokens, viacoins or XCH safely and easily. ClearingHouse matches and settles your trades automatically. ## Crowd-Funding Easily create a coin or token to promote your brand or crowd-sale. Distribute profits or dividends to your users. ## Block-Explorer You can see ClearingHouse transactions in process at [XCH BlockScan](http://xch.blockscan.com/) ## Source-code The source code can be found on the Github [ClearingHouse](https://github.com/ClearingHouse) project.
