# ClearingHouse ClearingHouse is a protocol that allows for asset issuance and p2p trading on the [Viacoin](http://viacoin.org) blockchain. Viacoin is a particularly fast digital currency which was designed to be the data-carrier for ClearingHouse, 25x faster than Bitcoin. It was released on 11th August 2014. You can read more about it on the Viacoin Blog: http://blog.viacoin.org/2014/08/11/1-clearinghouse.html ## XCH XCH is the native currency of ClearingHouse and is required to issue crypto assets and stocks. XCH cannot be mined. The total supply is fixed at 103,837,096.51 XCH. XCH can be traded on the DEx at [Clearwallet.co](https://clearwallet.co) and at [Bter](https://bter.com/trade/XCH_BTC) and [Poloniex](https://poloniex.com/exchange/btc_xch). ## Clearwallet [Clearwallet.co](https://clearwallet.co) is a web implementation that takes advantage of the ClearingHouse protocol. <iframe width="560" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/uIOjWI3tNr0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe> ## Decentralized Exchange Trade your own assets, tokens, viacoins or XCH safely and easily. ClearingHouse matches and settles your trades automatically. ## Crowd-Funding Easily create a coin or token to promote your brand or crowd-sale. Distribute profits or dividends to your users. ## Block-Explorer You can see ClearingHouse transactions in process at [XCH BlockScan](http://xch.blockscan.com/) ## Source-code The source code can be found on the Github [ClearingHouse](https://github.com/ClearingHouse) project.
